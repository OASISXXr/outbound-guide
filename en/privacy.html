Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

1. Who we are

OutboundGuide.org ("we", "us") is a non-official fan guide and wiki for the game Outbound. This policy explains what data we collect when you visit the site.

2. What data we collect

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to collect aggregated, anonymized data such as page views, country, device type, and browser version.
  • Heatmaps: We use Microsoft Clarity to collect anonymous interaction data (clicks, scrolls). No personally identifiable information is recorded.
  • Advertising: We may serve ads via Google AdSense. AdSense may use cookies to personalize ads. You can opt out at google.com/settings/ads.
  • Server logs: Our hosting provider may keep standard server logs (IP address, timestamp, requested URL) for security and debugging.

3. Cookies

The site uses cookies for analytics and advertising. By continuing to use the site you consent to the use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data: Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, Google AdSense, Cloudflare/GitHub Pages (hosting), YouTube (embedded videos).

5. Children

The site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the EU or California, you have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us via the Contact page.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent change.

About OutboundGuide.org

OutboundGuide.org is an independent, fan-built guide and wiki for the game Outbound, developed by Square Glade Games and released on Steam on May 11, 2026.

The site is run by a small team of survival-game enthusiasts who play, document, and verify in-game systems so other players can have a smoother first experience. We are not affiliated with Square Glade Games and do not represent the developer or publisher in any official capacity.

Our goal is simple: provide accurate, well-organized, bilingual (English + Chinese) guides that save players time during the early hours of the game.

What we cover

  • Starter / first-hour walkthroughs
  • Crafting recipes and unlock order
  • Energy, van layout and module guides
  • Multiplayer / co-op strategy
  • System requirements and FPS expectations
  • Patch notes, updates, and verified tips

How we verify content

Every recipe and system claim is tagged with a status: Confirmed (verified in-game with screenshots), Launch-day (being verified during the launch window), or Pending (sourced but not yet hands-on tested). We update our pages frequently, especially during the first month after release.

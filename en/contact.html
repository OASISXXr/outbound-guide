Contact

For all inquiries — corrections, takedown requests, partnership opportunities, guide contributions, or general feedback — please reach us at:

Email
contact [at] outboundguide.org

We aim to respond within 3 business days. For urgent IP / takedown requests, please include "URGENT" in the subject line.

What to include

  • Corrections: page URL + the specific text that is wrong + the correct version + your source.
  • Takedown: the rights you hold + the URL or asset in question + your contact info.
  • Contributions: what topic, your draft (Markdown or Google Doc), and how you'd like to be credited.
